Problem Statement:

Type: Open Statement Specific (Inunity)

Our innovative solution addresses the widespread challenge of patient non-adherence to medication, employing data science and machine learning for enhanced healthcare outcomes. Through advanced analytics, our web app predicts adherence challenges by analysing patient data, enabling proactive interventions. The platform establishes real-time communication channels between patients and healthcare providers, offering tailored support. With intuitive interfaces and reminders, it addresses common issues such as forgetfulness. This comprehensive, data-driven approach not only improves patient health outcomes but also equips healthcare professionals with valuable insights, reducing hospitalizations and system inefficiencies. The inclusion of a user-friendly web app ensures accessibility and ease of use, making it a practical and impactful solution for improving medication adherence in real-world healthcare settings.
